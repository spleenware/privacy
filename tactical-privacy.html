Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect personal data or transmit anything to the Internet. It only shares data (location and messages) among the team you setup, and only over the Ripple Tactical long range radio.

Location Data

The app captures the device’s location, even when in the background AND the long range radio is connected. If you disconnect the radio, then background location gathering ceases. The location data is never transmitted over the internet, nor is it stored on the device, by this app. The data is encrypted and transmitted to your Ripple Tactical team. Only team members who have exchanged keys with you can decrypt the data.

Service Providers

The app employs a third-party, namely Google, to provide maps to help visualise the location of your team members.

Security

Your data is only ever transmitted over the long range radio, and is encrypted with 256-bit ECC encryption. But remember that no method of transmission, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and I cannot guarantee its absolute security.
